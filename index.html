Mental Health Issues

Definition:

Mental health issues are a wide range of mental conditions
ranging from depressions to eating disorders. Its anything
that disordes you mood, thinking, and behavior. Genetics
can play a role in mental issues, however, they increase
the likeliness, it doesn't gurantee it.



Examples

  • Anxiety disorders
  • Behavioural and emotional disorders in children
  • Bipolar affective disorder
  • Depression
  • Dissociation and dissociative disorders
  • Eating disorders
  • Obsessive compulsive disorder
  • Paranoia

Solutions

Proffessional Solutions:

Solutions for mental health issues often involve Proffessional
treatment like therapy, medications, and presonal management.

Lifestyle & Care

Prioritizing sleep and eating a healthy diet, staying physcially active,
avoiding alcohol & drugs, connecting with nature, and practicing mindfulness
